Community Guidelines

Building a professional, safe, and trusted environment.

Our Commitment

Netixa is a professional community. We require all users to behave lawfully, respectfully, and kindly. These guidelines help ensure a safe space for everyone.

Do's

  • Be Professional: Share content that is relevant to careers, skills, and industry insights.
  • Be Authentic: Use your real identity and provide accurate information about your experience.
  • Be Respectful: Treat others with dignity. Constructive criticism is welcome; harassment is not.

Don'ts

  • No Hate Speech: We have zero tolerance for hate speech, discrimination, or threats of violence.
  • No Fake Profiles: Do not impersonate others or create fake accounts.
  • No Spam or Scams: Do not post misleading job offers, "get rich quick" schemes, or repetitive spam.
  • No Harassment: Do not bully, stalk, or harass other users.

Reporting Violations

If you see something that violates these guidelines, please report it immediately.
